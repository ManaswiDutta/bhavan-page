Dissonance

Swastik Maity,Physics,1st year

I have been drowning myself into depths of misery.

Sometimes knowingly sometimes not.


Hesitations, turned to reluctance, then shunned.

A revelation has occurred,

Let me cleanse myself of my vices,

Show me a path to forgiveness and peace.

The tranquil inner peace is in sight yet far away.

My selfish methods have left me perplexed

My rationale deluded me into futile complexities,

My eyes been shrouded by a veil of suspicion

I have been wary of depths while in shallows

With these tainted eyes I see around,

Melancholy prevails throughout

Someday an untainted pair would show me the path

To the Earth, to the heavens and to the hell ...

To get the essence of reality, thinkers have always wandered into murky depths of unreal.

Dissonance in my mind, discrepancy in thoughts.

May simplicity of thought prevail

And unnecessary complexities be shunned.
